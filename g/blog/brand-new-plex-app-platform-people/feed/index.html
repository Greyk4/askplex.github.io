@Robb: The home screen has “Recently Added” hubs for each media type, including movies. So, you can access recently added movies from the home screen. You can also select the movie library, choose ‘All Movies’ on the left, and then view all your movies. At the top there, you can filter and sort the content as desired (including sorting by date added). In the next update (already submitted to Roku), your filter/sort selection will also be remembered next time you enter the library. You could also filter/sort as desired in the web app (including sorting by date added) and save that as a smart playlist, which can be accessed on the Roku.
