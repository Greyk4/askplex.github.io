]]> By: Graeme Belle https://www.plex.tv/blog/welcoming-plex-on-samsung/#comment-46946 Sun, 27 Sep 2015 21:50:59 +0000 http://elan.plexapp.com/?p=1351#comment-46946 Bought my Samsung 65 inch UHD about three months ago. Chose it because it had the best image quality. I converted from a 50inch Plasma, so it will take for ever to acclimatise to a screen of grey clouds instead of flat black, trade-offs, power saving and heat reduction definitely major positives.

The PLEX app was a major surprise worked well, looked amazing and played mkv files.

I no longer need a “Media Portal” based PC connected to the TV. The PC/Server is now hidden in another room. The server gets quite busy when transcoding a couple of streams, now no more heat or noise in the media room which is windowless with air-con, major bonus.

I have since replaced my two WDTV boxes with Roku boxes running PLEX and have PLEX on everything else [5 PC’s 2 Android phones], truly amazing app!

There are several small annoyances that could be changed but everything works so well they’re no big deal:
*constant logins to activate my account (possibly because I use a VPN)
*database optimisation should be automatic or scheduled, with a backup option
*option to have TV boot to PLEX on startup
*recently added episodes list individually, not by season as on Roku
*subtitles take some fiddling

Overall 9 out of 10
