Organize, discover, and share

Once you add your photos and videos to Plex, they are organized in one place that is easy to navigate and beautiful to look at from any device. Create photo albums, share libraries, edit metadata, browse all of your photos and videos using automatic image- and geo-tagging, or rediscover forgotten memories.

The magic of automatic photo-tagging

Using object and scene recognition, Plex tags your photos based on subject (kids, sports, sunsets, etc.). Fine-tune your tags by removing, editing, or adding your own! Plex also supports Places, which uses any GPS data in a photo to show you where it was taken.

Timeline view

Chronological clusters make it easy to see your precious memories in the order they happened. Scroll through your photo collection, watching time go by as quickly or slowly as your fingers decide in our beautiful mosaic layout.
